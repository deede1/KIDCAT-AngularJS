Providing the tools and technology to power the library of the future

The library of the future is web-enabled, participatory and made of people. It is more than just book repositories; it is an information and community center. Auto-Graphics believes that the more libraries fully engage and make available their tools and information, the more central they become in the lives of their constituencies. Our products are designed to help libraries manage, share and search their resources.
